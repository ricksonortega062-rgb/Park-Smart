⚙️ What We Do

Built for the Future
of Urban Mobility

ParkSmart combines cutting-edge IoT sensors, real-time data processing, and intuitive software to transform how you park.

01

Real-Time Slot Detection

IoT sensors installed in each parking slot detect vehicle presence and transmit live status updates to our central platform every second.

02

License Plate Recognition

Automated QR and plate scanning enables seamless entry and exit without manual ticketing, reducing congestion at parking entrances.

03

Smart Reservation Engine

Our intelligent booking system ensures no double-bookings, manages waitlists, and automatically adjusts slot availability in real time.

04

Operator Management Portal

Parking lot operators get a powerful dashboard to manage slots, track revenue, view analytics, and configure pricing — all in one place.

05

Automated Billing & Reports

The system calculates fees automatically based on duration, sends digital receipts, and generates comprehensive financial reports for operators.

06

Mobile & Web Access

Access ParkSmart from any device — desktop browser or mobile. Our responsive platform ensures a consistent experience everywhere you go.
