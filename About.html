About ParkSmart

We're building
smarter cities

Founded in 2021, ParkSmart is on a mission to eliminate parking frustration through intelligent technology, real-time data, and thoughtful design.

Our Mission

Parking that
just works

We believe parking should be the least stressful part of your day. ParkSmart combines cutting-edge technology with an intuitive interface to make parking management effortless for both drivers and operators.

From real-time occupancy monitoring to advance reservations and automated billing, every feature we build is designed with a single goal: make parking better for everyone.

2021

ParkSmart Founded

Started with a vision to digitize parking management in Metro Manila.

2022

First 10 Lots Onboarded

Launched pilot program across 10 commercial parking facilities.

2023

5,000+ Users

Grew to thousands of active users and introduced the reservation system.

2024

50+ Lots, National Expansion

Expanded to major cities across the Philippines with new features.

2025

10,000+ Users & Growing

Launched advanced analytics, EV support, and AI-powered predictions.

Our Values
